How does Insurance work?

Insurance companies use a lot of data points to calculate your insurance premium, maximum possible cover amount as well as features of the insurance policy. They use various probability and forecasting models to analyze the demography, your current and historical health information, and related information to determine the possibility of providing you the insurance.

How to Choose India's Best Mutual Funds?

The best mutual fund for an investor is the one that generates the highest possible return. The investor's risk tolerance, time horizon, and life objectives are the three most important factors to consider before making a decision.
